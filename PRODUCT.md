# Kevin Marín — Portfolio

## Register
brand

## Product Purpose
Personal developer portfolio for Kevin Alexander Marín, Full Stack Developer based in Colombia. The portfolio showcases his work, skills, and experience to potential clients and employers. Design IS the product — it must communicate technical excellence through its own execution.

## Users
Potential clients with a freelance project (primary), technical recruiters and hiring managers (secondary). They arrive from LinkedIn or GitHub. They form a snap judgment in under 10 seconds. They need to trust that Kevin ships real, production-grade work.

## Brand
Precise. Minimal. Confident without being loud. Kevin builds systems that run in production for months — the portfolio should feel the same: zero unnecessary weight, everything earns its place. Not a creative agency portfolio. Not a startup landing page. A craftsman's work log.

## Tone
First-person, direct. No superlatives. Let the numbers speak ("1,400 transactions in 8 months" > "extensive production experience"). Spanish default; English on toggle.

## Anti-references
- Generic Tailwind starter templates
- Gradient-everything SaaS landing pages
- Bento-grid skill showcases with emoji icons
- "Hire me" hero copy that sounds like a LinkedIn summary

## Strategic principles
- Every design decision should make the code look serious
- The aurora background earns its place by creating atmosphere without competing with content
- Monochrome with blue accents — the palette of a terminal, not a marketing site
- Typography hierarchy > decoration
